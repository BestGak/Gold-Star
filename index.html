Welcome to Golden Star AC, your trusted partner for air conditioning and heating service and installation in Los Angeles!

Our company has been proudly serving Los Angeles and the surrounding areas for 7 years. During this time, we have established ourselves as a reliable and professional air conditioning and heating service provider. Because of our experience and top quality service, we are chosen by many customers in the area. Our technicians have extensive experience in servicing, repairing and installing a variety of air conditioning and heating systems. We are proud to have successfully completed over 15,000 repairs and installed over 5,000 systems, bringing joy and comfort to our customers.

Our story began on April 16, 2016, and since then Golden Star AC has continually grown and evolved, striving for excellence in everything we do. Our superior customer service and commitment to customer satisfaction is the driving force behind our company.
